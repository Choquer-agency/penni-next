Careers At Penni

Join Our Payment Processing Company

Penni is committed to helping businesses in Canada with our payment processing system.  
Join our team today to help build the payment processor of the future.

Help Us Change Lives with Low-Rate Payment Processing

At Penni we are committed to supporting a fun and creative work culture, made up of curious and detail-oriented individuals. You will play a large role in the success of businesses who need a point of sale system in Canada, by providing your experience and expertise in the industry. At Penni, we are re-imagining commerce in a big way, as our POS installation and processing company grows, so will the learning and education of our team. Get in touch about job opportunities and change the future of payments with Penni.

Join A Team That Is Making A Difference

Penni is looking for enthusiastic and passionate individuals to join our payment processing company in Canada. We encourage people with experience in the merchant payment processing industry to apply but we are open to those who possess related experience within each position. We are currently looking to fill the positions listed below.

Account Executive - Sales

Apply Today

Become an asset to Penni’s sales team as our account executive. This position will require an individual with an outstanding work ethic to formulate sale strategies, provide client support, and close sales. Our account executive must align with our purpose, helping businesses succeed with Penni Payments. We are looking for an individual with prior experience in a payment processing company, with a strong account executive resume.

Webflow Web Developer

Apply Today

We are looking to hire a web developer with experience working with Webflow. We are offering a competitive web developer salary in Canada with flexible hours. If you are a passionate individual and have experience with challenges in web application design and development, please submit your web developer cover letter and resume.

SEO Specialist

Apply Today

Penni is looking to invite an experienced SEO specialist to the team. This individual will utilize their expertise to grow Penni’s website through optimization and increase our lead generation. As the web marketing SEO specialist, you will implement your SEO specialist skills to perform keyword research, competition analysis and analytics management. Along with your resume, please submit an SEO specialist cover letter.
