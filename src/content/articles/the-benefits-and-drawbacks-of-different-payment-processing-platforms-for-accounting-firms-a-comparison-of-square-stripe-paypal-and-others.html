PCI Compliance: Important Information

‍

To safeguard cardholder data, the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) is an industry-wide security standard. To process payments securely, all platforms for payment processing must adhere to the PCI DSS. This comprises tools for fraud detection, risk management, chargeback protection, tokenization, user authentication, refunds and reversals, and dispute resolution.
