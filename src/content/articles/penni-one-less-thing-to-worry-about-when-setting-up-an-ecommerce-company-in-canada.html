What is online Payment Processing?

Online payment processing is the way your customers pay for products and services online. Payment processing includes all the steps involved in transferring money from a customer's credit card to your bank account. The process can be confusing, but we're here to help!

Why Do I Need it?

When you set up an ecommerce company in Canada, you might think that you can just accept credit card payments on your website without any kind of payment processing service, but that's not true! If you don't have a payment processor set up, you will not be able to accept credit card payments through your website. You'll also find that it's harder to manage your inventory, track sales, and invoice customers without a good system in place.

What are My Options?

When you’re setting up an ecommerce company in Canada, you’ll notice that there are many different types of payment processors and they all offer different features based on the type of plan you sign up for.

However, they all work in roughly the same way: you set up an account with them, then provide them with information about yourself and your business (like bank account numbers), and they take care of everything else. They collect money from customers' credit cards or other accounts and send it to your bank account.
