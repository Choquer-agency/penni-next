Pricing

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a credit card processor is pricing. Credit card processors typically charge fees for their services, which can include charges for equipment rental, software licenses, and transaction processing. It is important to compare the pricing of different credit card processors to ensure that you are getting the best deal.

Compatibility with Your Business Needs

It is also important to consider whether a credit card processor is compatible with your business needs. This includes factors such as the types of credit cards that the processor can accept, the types of transactions that the processor can handle, and the level of security that the processor provides.

Customer Service

Customer service is another important factor to consider when choosing a credit card processor. You want to choose a processor that is responsive and able to help you with any issues or questions that may arise.
